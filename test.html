MAKE TOOMMOROW FOR INNNOATION GENERATION(INDIAN FINALIST)

In December 2018, I had the honor of being selected as an Indian finalist for the Make Tomorrow for Innovation Generation program. This program recognized the top 50 projects from India and was held at the Manekshaw Centre in New Delhi. I attended the regional boot camp hosted by Intel and DST, and my project was ultimately chosen as one of the finalists. This recognition was truly rewarding, and I am grateful for the experience this program provided..

CBSE NATIONAL SCIENCE EXHIBITION (NATIONAL FINALIST & TOP 24 FROM EAST ZONE INDIA)

In January 2020, I was honored to be selected as a National finalist and one of the top 24 from the East Zone in India for my prototype project "Smart Water Management System". The competition was held at SunCity School in Gurgaon, Haryana. After being shortlisted as a regional winner, I had the opportunity to compete in the National competition and ended up being a runner-up. However, the biggest prize was the valuable lessons and experiences gained throughout the competition. I am grateful for the recognition and the opportunity to be a part of this program.
